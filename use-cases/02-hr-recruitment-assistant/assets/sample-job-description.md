## Position Overview

EGAT is seeking an experienced Senior Electrical Engineer to join our Power Plant Engineering team. The successful candidate will be responsible for electrical system design, maintenance planning, and ensuring reliable power generation operations across our facilities.

This role involves both office-based engineering work and on-site visits to power plants throughout Thailand.

---

## Required Qualifications

### Education
**Minimum**:
- Bachelor's degree in Electrical Engineering or Electrical Power Engineering
- Graduation from an accredited university

**Preferred**:
- Master's degree in Electrical Engineering, Power Systems, or related field

### Experience
**Required**:
- Minimum 5 years of professional experience in electrical engineering
- At least 2 years in power generation or heavy industrial facilities
- Experience with high-voltage systems (115kV or above)

**Preferred**:
- 8+ years total experience
- Experience in power plant operations or maintenance
- Previous work with EGAT or similar utility company

### Technical Skills (Required)
**Must Have**:
- Strong knowledge of electrical power systems
- Experience with protective relay systems
- Familiarity with transformer maintenance and testing
- Understanding of power generation equipment (generators, switchgear)
- Ability to read and create electrical schematics and single-line diagrams
- Knowledge of relevant Thai and international standards (IEC, IEEE, ANSI)

**Required Software**:
- AutoCAD or similar CAD software for electrical drawings
- MS Office (Word, Excel, PowerPoint)
- Power system analysis tools (ETAP, SKM, or equivalent)

### Technical Skills (Preferred)
- SCADA systems experience
- Power system simulation and modeling
- SAP PM (Plant Maintenance) module
- Experience with condition-based maintenance techniques
- Knowledge of renewable energy integration

### Language Requirements
**Required**:
- Thai: Native or fluent (reading, writing, speaking)
- English: Good (minimum TOEIC 550 or equivalent)
  - Able to read technical documents
  - Able to communicate with international vendors
  - Able to write technical reports

**Preferred**:
- English: Very good to fluent (TOEIC 750+)

### Certifications
**Required**:
- Licensed Professional Engineer (Thailand) OR ability to obtain within 1 year

**Preferred**:
- PMP (Project Management Professional)
- Professional Engineer License (Thailand)
- Any relevant vendor certifications (Siemens, ABB, GE, etc.)

### Other Requirements
- Valid driver's license (for site visits)
- Willingness to travel to power plant sites (up to 30% of time)
- Ability to work in industrial environments
- No health restrictions for working at height or with high-voltage equipment

---

## Key Responsibilities

### 1. Engineering Design & Analysis (40%)
- Design electrical systems for power plant modifications and upgrades
- Perform power system studies (load flow, short circuit, protection coordination)
- Review and approve electrical drawings and specifications
- Develop technical specifications for equipment procurement
- Ensure compliance with Thai and international standards

### 2. Maintenance Planning & Reliability (30%)
- Develop preventive and predictive maintenance programs for electrical equipment
- Analyze equipment failures and recommend improvements
- Support condition assessment of high-voltage equipment
- Participate in root cause analysis for electrical incidents
- Review and update maintenance procedures

### 3. Project Management (20%)
- Lead or support electrical engineering projects
- Coordinate with contractors and equipment vendors
- Monitor project budgets and schedules
- Ensure quality control and commissioning of new equipment
- Prepare project reports and presentations

### 4. Technical Support & Training (10%)
- Provide technical guidance to plant operations and maintenance teams
- Conduct or coordinate training sessions
- Serve as subject matter expert for electrical systems
- Mentor junior engineers
- Support troubleshooting of complex electrical issues

### Weighted Scoring (100 points total)

Candidates who pass knockout criteria are scored as follows:

#### Education (15 points)

- Master's degree: 15 points
- PhD: 15 points + bonus consideration

#### Experience (30 points)
- 5-6 years: 20 points
- 7-9 years: 25 points
- 10+ years: 30 points
- Power plant specific experience: +5 bonus points

#### Technical Skills (25 points)
- All required skills: 15 points
- CAD proficiency: 5 points
- Power system analysis tools: 5 points
- SCADA experience: +3 bonus points
- SAP PM experience: +2 bonus points

#### Certifications (15 points)
- Professional Engineer License: 10 points
- PMP or equivalent: 5 points
- Vendor certifications: +2 bonus points

#### Language Skills (10 points)
- Thai fluent: 5 points (required)
- English good (TOEIC 550-700): 3 points
